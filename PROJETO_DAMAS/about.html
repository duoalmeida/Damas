Checkers King

What's Checkers King?

It is a project developed by David Santos, Diego Nogueira and Vinicius Abril, with the main goal an alteration of the perspective the game Checkers, modifying a small part of the game rules, making it a little bit harder to the newbie players and even funnier for experienced players, always thinking to the amusement and using reasoning skills. King`s checkers goal is challenge players with different levels logic reasoning, giving them the capability to use them logic and perspectives instincts without losing all the fun.

Rules:

The Main rule and most important is to have fun, but we add a small challenge to you all, for now on the Queen only moves in lines horizontal and vertical respecting the limit of two moves from their starter point. Do not think that is so easy and we dare you to try it with your friends or with our pc boot.
